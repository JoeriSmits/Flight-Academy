Training

Step by step procedures

Training at the PreFlight-Academy is held individually on our dedicated training server which guarantees a fully controlled worldwide environment and airspace by the trainer. The Netherlands has a great airspace and we provide there mostly our trainings.

How much training do I get per month?

Your training sessions will be held twice a month, at most. In the time between the training you will have to do a lot of self-study. You will need to study your theory using the IVAO academy because their theory is specifically tailored to the IVAO theory exams.

Official certificate

We will conclude your training with an exam to test your practical skills. Once you have passed you will receive an official certificate and your IVAO account will be augmented with a VA profile that will show the rating in your personal information.

PREFLIGHT-ACADEMY

Course for those who find them self's struggling with flying using visual flight rules.

  • Communications
  • METAR
  • QDM / QDR on NDB and VOR
  • NDB bearing TO and FROM
  • Reading Charts
  • Levels and Altitudes
  • Aircraft equipment
  • Air Classifications

IRFLIGHT-ACADEMY

Course for those who want to learn the beginnings of IFR on a two prop plane.

  • Communications
  • IFR
  • METAR / TAF
  • VOR inbound / outbound
  • VOR sector intercept
  • Precision and non-precision approaches
  • Missed approach procedures
  • Reading Charts
  • Holding pattern (Direct Entry)
  • Levels and Altitudes
  • Aircraft equipment
  • Air Classifications

FLIGHT-ACADEMY

Course for those who want to learn advanced IFR and fly procedures. Will be flown in a multiple engine jetliner

  • Communication
  • METAR / TAF
  • VOR radial sector intercept
  • Precision and non-precision approaches
  • Missed approach procedures
  • IAF rules 90 / 120 degree
  • Reading Charts
  • ARC DME
  • Holding patterns standard
  • Holding patterns on DME fix
  • Procedure turns
  • Circling to land
  • Emergencies
  • Levels and Altitudes
  • Light configuration
  • Aircraft equipment
  • Air Classifications
