The Flight-Academy has existed for some time, but late 2009, it is official. Together with a professional team we provide an individually tailored training program in which personal support is key. Our trainings and exams are high-grade and are concluded with an official diploma.

We also provide courses on various subjects, such as "Radio Telecommunication"; "Reading Charts"; "METAR and TAF" where you may expect good support in your further virtual career.

  • Individually tailored training program
  • Professional team
  • High-grade
  • Personal support
